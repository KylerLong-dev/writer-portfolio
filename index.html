About

Doug Long is based along Florida’s Gulf Coast and often writes about nature and place and the world that defines who we are. Through his many travels and journals, he enjoys taking the reader to locations across the U.S.

He’s been published in short form in several literary magazines, websites and journals, including Lowestoft Chronicles, Saw Palms Literary Journal; featured in Lighthouse Digest with a work of historical fiction, and in Elsewhere - A Journal of Place, based in East Berlin.
